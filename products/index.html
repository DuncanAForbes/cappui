Our Digital Experiments + Products + Companies.

We search iteratively for Opportunities of value in data or AI/Machine Learning. Our initial focus at Cappui is around Platforms for Enterprise, Virtual Assistants and AR. Where we look to build two to three great products each year. Through proving the hypothesis of ideas that solve real problems. Challenging conventions + improving peoples lives. Bringing together creative and entrepreneurial minded talent. To craft, test and grow these ideas into products that can be turned into innovative, disruptive and profitable global companies.

Our Market Focus.

Below are the markets where we are brainstorming and researching ideas. Which we will prove a hypothesis for. And if the hypothesis can not be proven. We will continue iterating until we find a large unsolved problem. And then turn it into a prototype through the experimental phase fast. Working closely with our target customers as early on as possible. Then craft the product to beta to enter the release phase cycle of development. To turn it into a product that can be formed into a company when ready to enter its growth phase of life. Once we have determined how we can effectively and elegantly address a key market. To continue growing the product into a global company.

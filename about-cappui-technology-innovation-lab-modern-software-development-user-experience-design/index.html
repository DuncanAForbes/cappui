Crafting Great Products. Into Technology Companies

From proving the hypothesis of ideas to crafting products beyond beta into companies. We create products that are concepted to challenge and change the way people think, live and work. Collaborating with great companies through strategic partnerships as a collective to innovate and change global markets. As a startup studio.

We look to explore emerging technologies and test new ways for companies to use their information that benefit both their company's growth and the overall end-user's experience. Creating new models for improving information accessibility and company transparency. That focus on supply chain infrastructures, internet-of-things, financial data, energy and company reputation systems. Through harnessing a collective of strategic partners that allow us access to resources that help maximise the potential of our companies product development and create products of real value and growth.

Duncan founded Cappui back in October 2015. He is a developer who loves designing simple user experiences on top of complex distributed micro services. And more recently experimenting with decentralised systems.

After starting web development back in 2006 at his Family Business Forbes Rentals. Alongside their lead graphic designer. Where he drifted into marketing for some years eventually founding a lifestyle company. Yet after a few years focusing on this project he decided it was time to move back to what he loves full-time again. Crafting and designing software into great products. The languages he crafts software in are Python, ReactJS and GoLang. His more recent interests to date are playing with virtual reality and seeing its future potential for enterprise use cases and new consumer experiences. He is very much influenced by Japanese philosophy, art and literature. And loves to sail, mountain bike, and hike.

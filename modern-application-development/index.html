Modern Development

Architecting solutions that are modulistic and use the latest in distributed or decentralised technologies

Modern development of solutions requires a programmatic approach. This approach is fundamental to building durable solutions connected through micro-services spread across a distributed network for persistence and limited downtime. With continuous delivery to market and infinite testing cycles being paramount. The below explains our thought process and approach to handling the development of solutions for our products or clients.

Workflow >

Collaboration is key to Cappui’s growth. As technology evolves we must adapt to these changes fast. Being able to easily adopt them into our solutions. Our design process is concerned with simplicity and agile philosophies. Where the end-user comes first. And software must be an interactive and engaging experience.

⬎

Distribute >

The core foundations of our solutions may be architected as distributed systems that communicate through micro-services between one another. For high-availability and scaleability in mind. Or decentralised systems. For our commitment to researching and experimenting with emerging technologies.

⬐

Modular >

Solutions whilst distributed must connect to one another as smaller programs rather than one larger solution. We require our software to be built like a swiss army knife. Built in layers with the option to call indviduals applications in an abstract way

⬎

Test >

Part of our agile process and one where we like to remind ourselves no software is perfect. This requires us to continuously go through a process of test driven development (TDD). Where we must test, write code to fail, write production code to pass and then refactor.

⬐

Communicate >

Throughout development we must be able to clearly communicate code that easily communicates to other developers what it does. #through commenting our work with clarity similar to this. This is all but necessary for future developers to understand our work.

⬎

Record and Version >

We must immutably record the state of our changes and versions in the development cycle of our software. With development, testing, staging and production being clearly defined and moderated. To easily track and audit our work in an effective manner.

⬐

Continuous >

An extension of our test driven development and agile strategies. Development processes must be about continuously building, testing and releasing software through automated builds. Making it possible for us to change according to user feedback, market changes and shifts in business strategy.

⬎

Strategy >

To compete in global markets and craft innovative products that meet long term strategic visions. We must plan short and long term growth strategies that consider the monetisation of Cappui’s and client related projects. Where it is imperative we monitor competitive, industry and emerging technology trends. And how these may impact both Cappui or our clients.

⬐

Open Source >

Product development is about being open. Releasing much of our code to the world. To collaborate and enable those outside of Cappui to contribute to our projects. Through strategic partnerships and indvidual voluntary contribution. Enabling the creation of quality and innovative enterprise grade software that may compete in global markets.

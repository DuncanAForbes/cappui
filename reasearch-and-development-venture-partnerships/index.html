Our Process.

Part of our culture is to challenge ourselves everyday and be driven by our passion to create new experiences. Staying disciplined and focused doing our best everyday. To keep ourselves focused. Sticking to our companies design thinking and modern development prinicples. As well most notably continuing to learn new things daily. To improve our skillset.

Phase 1: Ideation.

During the first phase we research and develop ideas and concepts. Brainstorming and testing their hypothesis. Through a mix of focus groups with possible customers and market analysis. Where 8 out of 10 ideas are scrapped. Until we find a desirable and complex problem to address. Which we can simplify and solve.

The ideation stage is meant to allow us to identify markets that have opportunities. And are not yet being disrupted and could add real value when crafted and brought to market.

Phase 2: Incubation.

During the second phase we work in an extremely disciplined manner to craft products at speed. Although the aesthetic design is extremely important to us. We stick to a minimalistic approach both in user interface design and modern development. And we believe it important to get to know our customers throughout the development of the product.

The main goal for this phase is to test whether our hypothesis proves itself further and can be scaled into a profitable business.

Phase 3: Growth.

During the third phase we've collectively realised how to address the markets needs of our customers. Through strategic partnerships that include E.g resource or knowledge sharing. Then combine our market and operating knowledge with a dedicated team. And capital to scale from product to a growth business.

The growth phase's primary goal is to create companies that have a social, sustainable and environmental impact. Which add real value.

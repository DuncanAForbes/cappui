Products In Incubation Phase.

These are products we've proved the hypothesis for and are currently crafting.

Crafting The Futures Information Supply Chain Network Infrastructure. And Organising its Digital DNA.

Famoi is crafting a modern and decentralised information supply chain management platform. On top of Ethereum. To streamline product information between vendors and end-users. And help enterprises transition away from centralised and traditional or hybrid information management solutions. To minimise the risk of information leakage between competitors.

Making it simple to immutably track, monitor and trace inventory from their origin to end-user the consumer. And allow consumers to discover more information on the products they purchase. From where they originated, the conditions they where manufactured in to the product, store or company's reputation.
